Bo Li (李博)

About Me

I received the BSc and PhD degrees from the Department of Computer Science, Nanjing University, China, in 2014 and 2019, respectively. From 2020 to 2023, I served as Senior Researcher of Youtu Lab in Tencent, China. I am currently a senior expert at vivo image algorithm research department, China. My research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition and artificial intelligence.
